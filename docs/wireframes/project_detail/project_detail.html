The Challenge

Users struggled to visualize their long-term savings goals due to a cluttered interface. The existing data visualization was non-interactive, leading to a high drop-off rate on the 'Goals' tab.

lightbulb

The Solution

Introduced a gamified savings tracker with interactive charts. We simplified the information architecture to surface key metrics immediately upon login, using progressive disclosure for details.
